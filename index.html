My City: Salfit

Salfit is a Palestinian city located in the northern West Bank, serving as the administrative center of the Salfit Governorate. It is known for its fertile agricultural land, with olive cultivation being one of its main activities, producing some of the finest olive oil in Palestine. Despite its agricultural richness, the city faces challenges due to the presence of surrounding Israeli settlements, which hinder its urban and economic expansion. In recent years, Salfit has focused on improving its infrastructure and promoting local commerce, striving to overcome these obstacles and enhance the quality of life for its residents.
